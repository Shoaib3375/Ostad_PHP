Instructions:

 

Use Bootstrap to create a responsive navigation bar according to the design specifications provided.
Design Specifications:
a) Create a navigation bar that includes a logo and links to three sections: Home, About, and Contact.

b) The navigation bar should be responsive and collapse into a hamburger menu on smaller screens.

c) When the hamburger menu is clicked, the menu items should slide down.

d) The logo should be aligned to the left side of the navigation bar and the menu items should be aligned to the right side of the navigation bar.

The active menu item should be highlighted.
